Title: Anna Karenina
Author: Leo Tolstoy
Publication: Pan Books

Anna Karenina is a novel by the Russian author Leo Tolstoy, first published in book form in 1878. Widely considered to be one of the greatest works of literature ever written,[2] Tolstoy himself called it his first true novel.
